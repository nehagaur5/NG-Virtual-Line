Step-by-Step Breakdown

1

Arrive at the Shop

What You See:
  • • QR code displayed at the shop entrance or counter
  • • Shop name and current serving number visible
  • • Clear instructions: "Scan to join queue"
What You Do:
  • • Open your phone's camera app
  • • Point camera at the QR code
  • • Tap the notification to open the queue page

Pro Tip: No need to download any apps! Your phone's built-in camera works perfectly.

2

Join the Queue

Queue Information:
  • • Shop name automatically filled
  • • Current serving number displayed
  • • Estimated wait time shown
  • • Optional note field for special requests
Your Actions:
  • • Add optional note (e.g., "Extra spicy", "No onions")
  • • Tap "Get Token" button
  • • Receive your unique token number
  • • See your position in the queue

Real-time Updates: Your position updates automatically as the queue progresses.

3

Wait & Get Notified

Live Dashboard:
  • • Your token number prominently displayed
  • • Current serving number updates in real-time
  • • Estimated wait time recalculated
  • • Queue position and status visible
Smart Notifications:
  • • "Next in Queue" notification when you're up next
  • • "Now Serving" notification when your order is ready
  • • Browser notifications (with permission)
  • • In-app notification history

Freedom to Wait: You can browse nearby shops, sit comfortably, or do other activities while waiting.

4

Collect Your Order

When Notified:
  • • Go to the counter when you receive "Now Serving" notification
  • • Show your token number to the vendor
  • • Collect your order
  • • Confirm service completion in the app
Service History:
  • • All your orders are saved in history
  • • Track service times and wait periods
  • • View completed orders across all shops
  • • Rate and review your experience

Complete Experience: From joining the queue to collecting your order, everything is tracked and optimized.
